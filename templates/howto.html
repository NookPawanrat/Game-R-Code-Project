HOW TO PLAY

When the game starts, you will receive a ‘’Mission File’’, which can be opened by clicking on the file cover.

Open the ‘’Mission File’’ and you will see your tasks and task clues. Enter your answers into the answer box according to the clues and click ‘’Send’’. You will be given multiple missions until you successfully find where the criminal is.

You will see your current location and your name and ID in the upper right corner of the mission page. In the lower right corner you will see the status of the criminal's release of Ricina.

During the game, you can always check "How to Play" on the lower right to help you with the game.

Button of "Show All Countries" on the lower right can help you narrow down your answers.

Of course, you have the right to exit the task at any time, and the button of Exit on the lower right can help you. But we hope you can persist to the end, catch the criminals and stop the spread of Ricina.GOOD LUCK!!
